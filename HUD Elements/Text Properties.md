## TextColor Codes

- ^0  =  Black
- ^1  =  Red/Orange
- ^2  =  Lime Green
- ^3  =  Yellow
- ^4  =  Dark Blue
- ^5  =  Light Blue
- ^6  =  Purple
- ^7  =  White (Default)
- ^8  =  Black
